Cooking Guide

Master Pad Thai in 6 Steps

Thailand's most iconic noodle dish — learn the authentic technique used by street food vendors in Bangkok.

🍜

Classic Pad Thai

The authentic Bangkok street style

25
MINS
2
SERVINGS
485
CALORIES

🛒 Key Ingredients

Rice noodles 200g
Shrimp 150g
Tamarind paste 3 tbsp
Fish sauce 2 tbsp
Palm sugar 2 tbsp
Bean sprouts 1 cup
Spring onions
Roasted peanuts
1

Soak the Noodles

Soak rice noodles in room-temperature water for 30 minutes until pliable. Do not use hot water — this is the most common mistake beginners make.

2

Prepare the Pad Thai Sauce

Mix tamarind paste, fish sauce, and palm sugar in a bowl (3:2:2 ratio). Taste and adjust — a perfect Pad Thai sauce is sweet, sour, and salty in balance.

3

High-Heat Wok Technique

Heat your wok until smoking before adding oil. This "wok hei" effect creates the characteristic charred aroma of authentic street Pad Thai.

4

Cook Protein & Scramble Egg

Stir-fry shrimp until pink (1-2 min), push to the side, add egg and scramble into curds. Combine with the shrimp before adding noodles.

5

Toss Noodles with Sauce

Add drained noodles, pour sauce over top. Toss every 30 seconds, letting noodles absorb the sauce and caramelize slightly at the bottom.

6

Garnish & Serve Immediately

Top with bean sprouts, spring onions, roasted peanuts, lime wedge, and dried chili flakes. Serve with the traditional 4-condiment table set.
